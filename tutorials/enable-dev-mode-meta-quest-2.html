How to enable developer mode on the Meta Quest 2



By streetlevr

Step 1

Create a developer account

Go to the Oculus Developers page and log-in with the account you used to sign into your Meta Quest 2 on your phone.

Step 2

Verify your account

Verify your account using a Credit Card or by enabling Two-Factor Authentication.

Two-Factor Authentication requires a phone number, you cannot use an authenticator app.


Step 3

Make an organization

Create an organization. The name does not matter.



Step 4

NDA

Agree to the Non-Disclosure agreement

Step 5

Connect your headset

Open the Oculus App on your iPhone or Android and connect your Meta Quest 2 via Bluetooth



Step 6

Enable Developer Mode
